# FacturaGuard Marketing Notes

## Positioning

FacturaGuard is a B2B SaaS for Romanian companies and accounting offices that need safer RO e-Factura monitoring.

## Primary buyer personas

1. Accounting offices managing multiple client companies.
2. SMEs that issue invoices and need compliance visibility.
3. Financial/admin teams that need audit-ready reporting.

## Core promise

Stop discovering e-Factura problems too late.
